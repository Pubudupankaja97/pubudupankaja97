About Pubudu

Hi, I'm Pubudu
Graphic
Designer

I’m Pubudu Senavirathne, a dedicated Quantity Surveyor turned creative professional with a passion for design and storytelling. I hold a BSc (Hons) in Quantity Surveying from De Montfort University, graduating with Second Class Upper Honours. After gaining experience in the construction industry, I transitioned into my true passion—Graphic Design and Content Creation.

Currently, I work with First Team Group, a diverse conglomerate with ventures in Real Estate, Construction, Tourism, Hospitality, Advertising, and Software Development. In my role as a Graphic Designer and Multimedia Specialist, I bring ideas to life through impactful visuals and engaging content. I’m committed to growing within this dynamic group while continuing to blend creativity with strategy.
