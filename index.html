Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions clients ask about my Graphic Designing and Social Media Management services.

What services do you offer in Graphic Designing?

I provide a wide range of design services including logo design, branding, packaging, social media post designs, business cards, flyers, and more — all customized to match your brand’s personality and goals.

Do you handle social media management too?

Yes, I do! I manage complete social media pages — from designing posts and writing captions to scheduling, engaging with audiences, and tracking performance to help your brand grow online.

How long do you take to design a logo or brand identity?

It usually takes me around 3–5 working days to design a professional logo and about 7–10 days for a full brand identity package, depending on your requirements and feedback.

Do you create custom designs or use templates?

All my designs are fully custom-made. I don’t use pre-made templates — every project I work on is unique and created from scratch to reflect your brand’s identity.

Can you help me grow my followers and engagement?

Definitely! I focus on creating consistent, attractive, and engaging content that helps increase reach, followers, and audience engagement — both organically and through ad campaigns if needed.

What do you need from me before starting a project?

I’ll need some basic details like your business name, target audience, design preferences, color scheme, and any ideas or sample references you already have. The more details you share, the better I can match your vision.
